How Ranking Works

How You Earn Points

  • Getting Started: The first 10 people to submit prompts each get 5 points
  • Going Deeper: When you improve a branch that's already been improved, you earn bonus points. The more levels deep you go, the more points you get!
  • Making It Better: If you significantly improve someone's work, you get 3 times the improvement score as bonus points
  • Taking Ownership: When you create the best version of a branch, you become the owner and get 10 bonus points
  • Legacy Rewards: When someone builds on your work and makes it better, you get 25 points for being the foundation

Competitive Branch Ownership

Prompts are like contests - whoever gets the best evaluation score owns it. When someone improves a branch, ownership transfers and they get points. Previous owners are rewarded for creating the foundation.
