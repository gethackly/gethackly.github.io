Frequently Asked Questions

Hackly is a collaborative Discord server where developers of all skill levels come together to build open source projects. It's a space focused on learning through practical collaboration and real-world problem solving.

Anyone interested in development can join! Whether you're self-taught, a student, or already working in tech, Hackly welcomes developers of all levels. The community thrives on diverse perspectives and skill sets.

Projects typically run for 1-2 weeks, where teams collaborate to build quick prototypes. The focus is on creating versionable solutions that can potentially evolve into practical, open-source projects. Teams work together to solve real problems while learning from each other.

Projects range from web applications to tools and utilities that solve real problems. The emphasis is on creating practical, open-source solutions that can benefit the wider community. All projects are collaborative and open to contribution.

Simply join our Discord server using the link above! Once you're in, introduce yourself in the introductions channel and explore the different project channels. Feel free to ask questions and get involved in ongoing discussions.

No, Hackly is completely free to join! We believe in making development and collaboration accessible to everyone. The only requirement is your enthusiasm to learn and contribute.
