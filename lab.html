How to Use

Quick Guide

  1. Write your prompt in the editor below
  2. Use "===" to separate:
    • Before: Your requirements
    • After: Expected output
  3. Check your scores above

Scores Explained

Accuracy (45%): How well the output matches requirements

Complexity (35%): Technical depth and sophistication

Reliability (20%): Code quality and robustness

Tips

  • Be specific in requirements
  • Include example outputs
  • Specify technical needs
